Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

letter to C.V.CT.Venkatachalam Chettiar

Dr.RM. Alagappa Chettiar

'Alagappa College P.O.
Karaikudi.

          30th July, '48..

My Dear C.V.CT.

Rather I should address you as "C.V. Chettiar" in conformity with the affectionate manner in which Chettinad ladies call you and, latterly, our Premier, Omandur himself. I am indeed most grate- fully and genuinely moved by your unstinted labour of love. My only hope and prayer is that the friendship that has borne such great and delicious fruits, will continue to grow from more to more, andyour attachment give me anchorage during my moments of stress and turmoil and, at other times, the basis for building up great institutions, which will cause joy and happiness to many around us.

With kind regards and best wishes,

Yours very sincerely,
Sd..RM. Alagappa Chettiar

Sri C.V.CT. Venkatachalam Chettiar Av
Kanadukathan.
