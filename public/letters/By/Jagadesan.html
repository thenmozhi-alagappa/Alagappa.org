Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

letter to Shri T.N. Jagadesan

Dr.RM.Alagappa Chettiar 

'Krishna Vilas'
Vepry
Madras - 7

Dear Mr. Jagadisan,

I hasten from my sick-bed to send you my most genuine congratulations on the recognition by Government of your valuable services by the award of 'PADMASRI'. you are one of those souls that believe in doing your bit for humanity and in the true Gita Spirit carrying on your work in the spirit of 'Nishkhaamya Karma'. As I was dictating this letter, a number of friends were asking me who this Jagadesan was. It gave me the opportunity and the privilege of speaking out what you were, what you are and what you stand for, -rid particularly your close associationship wish Bapuji and Rajkumariji, and leaders and workers of that class and dignity. I count myself a happy man indeed in that I have built up your friendship and what is more ripened it in ever-growing pleasure all these years. Kindly accept my sincerest congratulations once again if you attach value to congratulations at all.

With kindest regards, Yours affectionately,

Sd...RM.Alagappa Chettiar

Shri T.N. Jagadesan
Hind Kusht Nivaran Sangh
"Windsor"
Edward Elliot’s Road,
Mylapore,
