Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

letter to Sri Sa. Ganesan

Dr.RM.Alagappa Chettiar

Alagappa College P.O

        30th July, 48

My dear Sa. Ganesan,

A kindly Providence has brought us close together in recent years, and I am indeed proud of the friendship that I have built in you. The functions, both of the College and later, of the Foundation Stone, were quite supreme in their respective lines; and not a little of the grandeur and the magnificence of these functions was due to the great affection that I have had the privilege to receive from an intimate circle of friends like you. My only prayer is that we should all march together through thick and thin to achieve the great and laudable objectives that we have in view, i.e., to build up a congregation of academic units imbued with the highest ideals.

With kind regards,

Sri Sa. Ganesan, Avl.,
Karaikudi.

Yours very sincerely,

Sd
RM.Alagappa _ Chettiar
