Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

letter to Sir. M.A. Chidambaram Chettiar

Dr.RM.Alagappa Chettiar

'Krishna Vilas'
Vepry
Madras - 7

       20th November, 54

Dear Mr. Chidambaram,

I hasten to congratulate you on the great and signal honour that is about to be conferred on you; the Mayoralty of the great city of Madras and it must be a source of satisfaction and great pride for the Three sons of that eminent man Raja Sir Annamalai Chettiar to become Mayor one after the other of our Historic City. It is of great significance that the list of Mayoralty was started of by your brother Raja Sir Muthiah Chettiar. Kindly do accept my most sincere congratulations. This is an honour of which anyone could properly be proud of.

Sri M.A. Chidambaram Chettiar
Mayor of Madras
Chettinad House
Adayar, Madras.

Yours sincerely,

(Sd.) RM.Alagappa _ Chettiar
