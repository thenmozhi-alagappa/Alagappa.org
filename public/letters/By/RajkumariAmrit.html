Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

letter to Rajkumari Amrit Kaur

Dr.RM.Alagappa Chettiar 

'Krishna Vilas'
Vepry
Madras - 7

       February 16, 53

Dear Rajkumari,

       I have the privilege of sending you my invitation for the inauguration of the building scheme of our Engineering College by President, Dr. Rajendra Prasad, on the 19th February. I have often been dreaming of inviting you to Karaikudi and bless my academic 'adventure' as Rajaji put it. I shall now be content by receiving your kind message and good wishes for the function and shall patiently wait for a day when you could kindly come personally and look around the various efforts that we are so earnestly attempting.

        With Kind regards,

Yours sincerely,

Sd...RM.Alagappa Chettiar

Hon'ble Rajkumari Amrit Kaur
Minister For Health
Central Secretariat
NEW DELHI.
