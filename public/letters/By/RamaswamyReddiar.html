Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

letter to Sri O.P.Ramaswamy Reddiar

Dr.RM.Alagappa Chettiar 

Alagappa College P.O.

       30th July 48.

Dear Sir,

I have been the recipient of your affection and sympathy all along. The cause with which I am associated has received impetus, strength and grandeur by tea- son of your blessings and Governmental help. I am most grateful to you and to your colleagues for having come down to Karaikudi and honored me and the institution with your presence. I am also deeply sensible of the fine words of encouragement that you spoke that day. May Providence give me the strength of mind and wisdom to assist in the completion of the great task which has been started under the blessings of our Greatest and Dearest.

With kind regards, , ,  

Yours very sincerely,

Sd...RM.Alagappa Chettiar

The Honourable Sri O.P.Ramaswamy Reddiar
Premier of Madras
Fort St.George,
Madras.
