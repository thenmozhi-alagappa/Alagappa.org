Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

Letter from Dr.C.P.Ramaswami Aiyer

Dr.C.P.Ramaswami Aiyer

The Grove
Teynampet, Madras

8/1/1957.

My dear friend,

That you should, from your sick-bed, turn your attention to the details of my comments on Dr. Toynbee's discourse, and that you should embody in your observations not only your rare genius for friendship but your profound reflections on our philosophy and its genesis and import, furnish one more proof, if proof were needed, of the marvelous resilience of your spirit and your alterness of mind.

That you may be soon restored to health and normalcy is the desire and prayer of.

Yours sincerely,

C.P. Ramaswami Aiyar.
Dr. Alagappa Chettiar,
Krishna Vilas,
Vepery,
Madras.
