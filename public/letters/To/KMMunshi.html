Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

Letter from K.M. Munshi

Covernor's Camp
Uttar Pradesh

        February 8, 1954

My dear Dr. Alagappa Chettiar,

I am very glad to receive your letter of February,2, inviting me to the ceremony of laying the Foundation stone of Shanmugham Library. He was also a valuable friend of mine and you could not have done better than associating his name with the Library.

I wish the function every success.

Yours sincerely,

Sd.. K. M. munshi
Dr. RM. Alagappa Chettiar,
Alagappa College P.O.
Karaikudi.
