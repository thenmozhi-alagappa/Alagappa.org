Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

Letter from G.D.Birla

G.D. Birla 

8 Royal Exchange Street
Calcutta.

       28th December, 55

Dear Mr. Chettiar,

Thanks for your letter of the 22nd December.

I am now going to Europe shortly. But when return, I will see how I could fit in a tour to the South in my itinerary.

I hope you are well now. With kindest regards,

Yours sincerely,

Sd...G.D. Birla
