Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

Letter from K.R.K. Menon

INDUSTRIAL FINANCE CORPORATION OF INDIA

new Delhi
Bangalore

       21st September 1953

New Delhi 23rd April 56. My dear Alagappa Chettiar, Just a line to acknowledge your letter and to confirm our subsequent telephonic conversation on the m0rning Of my departure for Bombay on tour. I do hope you are getting better and I pray God you may be restored to normal health very soon. I shall certainly try and do my best in the matter you spoke to me about.

With best regards and all good wishes,

  Yours sincerely,

Sd.. K.R.K. Menon
P.S. Please tell C.V.Ct. that I had his letter, and I shall be writing to him soon.
