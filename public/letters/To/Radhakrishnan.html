Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

Letter from S. Radhakrishnan

Vice President of India

New Delhi

23 January 1956..

Dear Dr. Alagappa Chettiar,

I am sorry to know about your illness are better now. I hope you I have received a copy of your letter addressed to the Ministry of Education and shall find out what the position is.

With kind regards,

Dr.Rm.Alagappa Chettiar,
"Krishna Vilas", Vepery,Madras 7. 		Yours sincerely,

Sd...S. Radhakrishnan
