Dr.Chettiar's Correspondence

Letter from Mr.Samuel Mathai

Samuel Mathai

University Grants Commission,
Old Mill Road, New Delhi 2.

D.0. No. D 5128/55(b) November 30, 1955

Dear Dr. Alagappa Chettiar,

Thank you very much for your letter of the 28th November and the copy of the Souvenir brochure and other papers enclosed with it. 1 have look through them with great pleasure. I look forward very much to the pleasure of visiting Karaikudi some day. As Karaikudi is not a University I shall have to have some special excuse for coming there but perhaps the excuse can be found before long.

With kind regards,

Yours sincerely,

Sd.. Samuel Mathai.
Dr. Alagappa Chettiar,
Krishna Vilas,Vepery,Madras.
